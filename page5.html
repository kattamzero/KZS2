What is Kattam Zero?

The Kattam Zero Store is range of products spreads happiness in the hearts of the people who play with them and are loved and played by one and all for generations.

we bring smiles

Vision:

To become a global leader in the toy business arena, create a nurturing place to work, bring a joyous smile on the faces of every child, create opportunities of employment in the society and persist in the journey of growth.

Mission

Contribute superior quality, technology and research-based product to the society and empower growth of the nation

Core Value

Ethical & Transparent Business Practice Working Together with Passion Commitment to Customers and Stakeholders Quality Consciousness in all Aspects Caring for Employees, Society and Environment Continues Learning and Growth

Business Philosophy

Our Business philosophy is to serve our valuable customers with best of the best solutions for their requirements by not only selling the products but also to develop and evaluate the sources for the most cost-effective solutions, keeping the quality standards at par with the benchmark.

We believe in ethical practice in every area of business starting from material-suppliers, employees, stakeholders, government authority to customers who are the foundation of our business.
